## The Problem

Daily, thousands of articles get published and forgotten. There's no permanent way to preserve the truth, verify who shared it, and no reward for the people who gives quality information. In media TRUST is broken. Wrap-Up builds the missing TRUST layer.

## Our Solution

**Wrap-Up** lets anyone paste a URL, get an AI-generated deep analysis, and mint that curation permanently on Starknet. Every article, research report, and comparison lives on-chain тАФ with community votes, comments, and curator reputation all verifiably recorded. No middlemen. No black boxes.

---

## тЬи What It Does

### ЁЯдЦ AI Research Engine
Drop any URL. Groq AI (Llama 3.1) scrapes and produces a full intelligence brief тАФ quick summary, detailed synthesis, key takeaways, and extracted statistics тАФ in seconds.

### ЁЯФм Multi-Source Research Reports
Generate cross-platform research reports that pull from web, academic, Reddit, and news sources simultaneously, then synthesize consensus vs. contradiction across all of them.

### тЪЦя╕П Article Comparator
Paste two URLs and get an 8-dimension AI battle: credibility, depth, bias, truthiness, impact, writing quality, public presence, and originality тАФ with a final on-chain verdict.

### тЫУя╕П Starknet-Powered Curation
Every article, research report, and comparison is minted to Starknet via Cairo smart contracts. IPFS (Pinata) stores the metadata permanently. Nothing disappears.

### ЁЯТм On-Chain Discussion
Comments and replies are uploaded to IPFS and posted on-chain. Upvoting is a blockchain transaction. Every vote is immutable and auditable.

### ЁЯПЖ Points & Token Rewards
Curate articles тЖТ earn points. Mint research тЖТ earn more. Get upvoted тЖТ earn even more. Claim $WUP tokens proportional to your accumulated on-chain contributions.
